"One Heart, One Way"

The Trastamara Sisterhood is a close-knit sorority of passionate and driven women. We are dreamers, romantics, and idealists. We are adventurers, seekers, and explorers. We seek answers for questions we have yet to ask. We take the first step on a path we do not yet see. We are driven by will, following our heart wherever it takes us. We are free.

Our Main Beliefs

Happiness is borne out of life and grown by love.

To love life means living yours to the fullest.

The meaning of love is found not in ourselves, but in one another.
