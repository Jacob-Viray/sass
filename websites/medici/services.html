Medici Doctrine

  • Competition starts with oneself. Test your own boundaries before attempting to test that of others.
  • A vision is made real by passion, articulation, and relentlessness.
  • Performance is the measuring stick to which we must all conform.
  • Always expect the unexpected.
  • Our potential is limited only by the human condition.

Campus Policies

  • These are the scholastic standards set by the student council association, SAIT-SCA.
  • Each member must have a minimum average GPA of 2.5 at the start of every semester.
  • Each fraternity or sorority should have a minimum average GPA that is above the province-approved limit at the start of every semester.
  • Any and all officers or official representatives of the fraternity, sorority, or Greek Affairs Board must have a minimum average GPA of 2.5 at the start of every semester.
  • All chapters and their individual members are subject to the standard Drugs and Alcohol Policy as well as the Risk Management Policy put forward by the province of Alberta.
