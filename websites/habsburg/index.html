"The Ties That Bind"

The Habsburg Sorority is a worldwide association founded on the principles of diplomacy, friendship, and unity. The organization and its 10,000 members welcome you to sit and parlay with us.

Core Beliefs

Diplomacy is an act of restraint, patience, and understanding.

Empathy is the resonance between two people.

Cooperation, not confrontation, is the building block of society.
