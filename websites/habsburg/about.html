Humble Beginnings

Interestingly, with all its preachings of peace and cooperation, The Habsburg Foundation was founded because of the first World War. It began as a gathering of a small band of army nurses who saw firsthand the evil that people could inflict on one another. Despite their humble backgrounds, these nurses sought to change the world.
